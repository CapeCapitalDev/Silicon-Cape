## CAPE in one paragraph

CAPE Capital is a Zurich-based family office and wealth manager. William Bowdler-Raynar
identifies major public-market themes and Moonshots, tests them rigorously, and converts
them into investable views and products. The research engine runs on LLMs, with interns
(Oscar for equity screening/RBICS, Ludovico for Python automation) supporting the workflow.
Weekly rhythm: one theme or Moonshot studied top-down to equity level, one presentation
produced, one summary page filed.

Live product: Cape Equity Moonshots Portfolio (AMC, UBS, ISIN CH1496790390, USD, 0.75%).
Investment mandate: listed public markets, international equities, thematic and
innovation-driven. Excluded: oil & gas, tobacco, weapons/defence.
